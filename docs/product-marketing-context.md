# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: February 27, 2026*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Federal startup job board for the nation of Canada

**What it does:** Searches for and creates a searchable directory of jobs at funded startups (pre-seed and beyond) for companies owned (invested in + founded by) and operated (HQ physically inside Canada) by Canadians. Serves two primary end-users: data analysts aggregating labour market data (there are 45,000 unfilled SWE jobs!) and job seekers looking for roles at Canadian startups.

**Product category:** Canadian Startup Jobs / Startup Jobs in Canada / Startup Jobs in [Canadian City]

**Product type:** Service (funded by NGO "Build Canada")

**Business model:** NGO funded, doesn't need to recoup. Used as a campaign tool for NGO-aligned (Pro-Growth) political candidates and to gather attention to the Build Canada organization.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Funded startups (pre-seed and beyond) that are owned (invested in + founded by) and operated (HQ physically inside Canada) by Canadians. Sourced from vetted list of Capital Sources (VCs, Banks, Family Offices) portfolios.

**Decision-makers:**
- Job Seekers (individual SWEs and tech workers)
- Analysts (policy analysts, economic analysts, immigration policy researchers)
- HR Departments (hiring managers, recruiters, HR professionals)

**Primary use case:** Defragment the Canadian startup labour market — "I want to find every open job listing that matches my skills in my country right now" and "I want to see competitive offers and quickly base my reqs/offers to the Canadian market."

**Jobs to be done:**
- **Job Seekers:** Find job listings to apply for & monitor the market
- **Analysts:** Source job data for Build Canada's "Data Warehouse" project for indexing, economic performance analysis, trends, and talent needs
- **HR:** Source job data for competitive benchmarking and offer positioning

**Use cases:**
- When I'm an unemployed Canadian SWE, I want to be able to search the keyword "typescript" and see every job that is looking for my expertise in that skill.
- When I'm an analyst working on immigration policy, I want to be able to see how many unfilled SWE jobs are in the country and how long they have stayed open.
- When I'm an HR pro writing a job description, I want to look at my competitors offers and make an offer that's comparable.

*Note: Job seeker is the primary UI user; analysts and HR are secondary users for data modelling.*

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Job seekers must job-hunt for weeks to months, manually reviewing all public job listings across the country because there's no central coverage on what startups even exist. Startups aren't celebrated in Canada like they are in SF.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- **LinkedIn / Indeed:** Disincentivized from actually helping users find work/hire — they need long engagement times to sell ad inventory
- **Headhunters:** Not capable of taking on out-of-network workers
- **US startup job boards (Y Combinator, WellFound, startups.gallery):** Don't include Canadian businesses
- **ATS systems (Workday, Greenhouse, etc.):** Don't offer job discovery systems

**What it costs them:**
- Job seekers spend weeks/months manually searching across scattered sources
- Missed opportunities for both workers and employers
- Policy decisions made without data (people "shout past each other on immigration without data")
- Canadians suffer economically
- Economic anxiety causes social issues
- R5 rate is 7.0

**Emotional tension:** Frustrated, lost, anxious

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Comprehensive coverage of Canadian startups
- Verified Canadian ownership and operation
- Data warehouse integration for analysts and policymakers
- Free/NGO-funded without ad inventory incentives

**How we do it differently:** Source from VC portfolios and monitor identified Canadian companies for new job listings — always fresh data.

**Why that's better:** This is an "Elbow's Up!" meme response to the U.S. v World Tariff War.

**Why customers choose us:** No ulterior motives, just fresh national data.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Is this comprehensive? How do I know it's up to date?" | Show live job counts, last-updated timestamps, and portfolio sources |
| "Is this legitimate? Who's behind it?" | Highlight Build Canada NGO backing, VC partners, and early adopters |
| "Will these jobs still be open when I apply?" | Link directly to the listing on the company page — users apply through the ATS as normal |
| "Why should I use this instead of LinkedIn?" | Canadian-specific focus, no fake listings, no ad inventory incentives |
| "Is this just for software engineers?" | All roles at VC-scale Canadian startups are included — tech and non-tech |

**Anti-persona:**
- Job seekers outside Canada
- Workers at established corporations/large enterprises
- Companies seeking US/international talent
- Part-time or freelance workers
- Non-VC-scale companies (must be pre-seed+ with capital backing)

## Switching Dynamics
**Push** (What drives them away from current solutions):
- Weeks/months of manual searching across scattered sites
- Fake listings on LinkedIn/Indeed (ad inventory incentives)
- Missing jobs because no central Canadian startup directory exists
- Thin referral networks that burn out quickly
- Economic anxiety & unemployment stress

**Pull** (What attracts them to this job board):
- One place to see every Canadian startup job
- Verified Canadian ownership/operation
- Fresh data from VC portfolio monitoring
- Free with no signup required — just search, filter, scroll
- Links directly to ATS (apply where comfortable)

**Habit** (What keeps them stuck with current approach):
- Already have LinkedIn profiles/networks
- Familiar search patterns on Indeed/LinkedIn
- "Everyone uses LinkedIn for jobs" (bandwagon effect)
- Don't know this exists

**Anxiety** (What worries them about switching):
- "Is this comprehensive? Will I miss jobs?"
- "Is this legit? Is Build Canada real?"
- "Will it stay updated? Will I waste my time?"
- No signup/payment required eliminates most anxiety
